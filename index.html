Our Values


VISION: Our vision is that the average human will be able to effortlessly automate their daily lives exactly to their tastes and preferences.


MISSION: AI and iOT products are becoming an essential part of the modern day human experience and with them so is the access the general public has to advanced automation. Virilio Automation will bridge the gap between human and technology by providing clean open source blueprints that anyone can color in to adopt automated tech into their lifestyles. We chose to start with a home automated system as our initial product is because it demonstrates the intrinsic value of enabling human preference with technology. Our mission is to make the most user friendly and customizable automation experience with a community of users who enrich the overall experience with their own.
