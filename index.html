Our Values


VISION: Our vision is that the average human will be able to effortlessly automate their daily lives exactly to their tastes and preferences.


MISSION: AI and iOT products are becoming an essential part of the modern day human experience and with them so is the access the general public has to advanced automation. Virilio Automation will bridge the gap between humans and technology by providing clean open source blueprints that anyone can color in to adopt automated tech into their lifestyles. We chose to start with a home automated system as our initial product because it demonstrates the intrinsic value of enabling human preference with technology. Our mission is to make the most user friendly and customizable automation experience with a community of users who enrich the overall experience with their own.

What we offer

Current Projects


Application

Our application will serve as the control hub for anything and everything we automate. Initially, it will be catered to our nutrient dispensing system where our users will be able to use the app to control the distribution, timing, and allocation of nutrients. Later on the control hub will be scaled as we create more automated products.

Nutrient Dispenser

We chose a nutrient dispenser as our launch product because it serves as an example of the value behind being able to automate something based on user preference and choice. Users will be able to fully automate how and what nutrients their plants are fed without having to do more then modify some settings as they see fit.

Plant Monitoring

The plant monitoring system will be trained to extrapolate information based on its observations. We decided to add this feature to so that people can monitor their plants from afar and remotely make changes to the way the plants are being taken care of based on the information our system presents to them.
